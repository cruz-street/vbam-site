01 · Positioning

A grown-up sibling, on purpose.

Vero Beach Pediatrics raised this town's children. Vero Beach Adult Medicine is the practice for the adults they grew into — and for the parents and professionals who chose Vero because the natural beauty quietly runs everything here.

Same family. New chapter.

Not a clinic at the end of a hospital hallway. A practice. With doors that open onto the same light Vero locals already love, and a panel small enough that your doctor remembers your last conversation, not just your chart.

02 · Founding Physician

Meet Dr. Patricia Stewart.

Board-Certified · Internal Medicine 12+ Years on the Treasure Coast Native Floridian

Dr. Stewart founded VBAM to practice medicine the way she always wished she could — slower, more personal, and built around the patient instead of the schedule. She trained at Emory, completed her residency at the University of Miami, and chose Vero because she believes great primary care belongs in the places people actually want to live.

"I want my patients to feel like they're already at ease the moment they walk in."
