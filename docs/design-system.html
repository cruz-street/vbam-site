09 · Philosophy Principles (3-col grid)
i.
Quiet, capable, unhurried.

Same-day callbacks. Same-week visits. A panel small enough that your doctor remembers your last conversation, not just your chart.

ii.
Warm, not clinical.

Light-filled rooms. Coastal art. A practice that reads like a hospitality brand — because that's how primary care should feel in 2026.

iii.
Local, never generic.

Real relationships with the specialists, hospitals, and labs Vero patients already trust. Care that fits your life on this coast.
